About Us / Our History

Our goal is to make recycling E-waste easier and simpler.

Aware Waste was founded by Arfa Abdur Rahim in 2023. We aim to target E-waste, and bring in measures that will make is easier for you and your family. Have any old devices laying around, and not sure how to recycle them? Worry not, Aware Waste is here to handle all your demands. We have recyled more that 700 devices, and our aim is to recycle 2500+ devices by December 2024.
